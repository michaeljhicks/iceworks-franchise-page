Dependable Programs

Why Lease an Ice Machine?

Owning and maintaining an ice machine can be costly and labor-intensive. There are many benefits to leasing an ice machine in Phoenix and throughout Arizona instead of purchasing one outright.

Consistent Supply Guaranteed

Leasing with us is the easiest way to provide your business with ice. With our Worry-Free Ice Guarantee, if your machine ever goes down, we will deliver ice straight to your door or reimburse you.

Maintenance Included

Maintenance, cleanings, and repairs are included in the monthly fee. You never have to worry about unexpected repair costs or finding a qualified technician to service your machine.

Expert Support

Our dedicated team is committed to addressing issues proactively to ensure minimal impact on operations. We ensure your machine is always in top condition.

Customizable Leasing

Every business is unique, which is why we offer customizable leasing plans. Whether you need a small machine for a boutique hotel or a large machine for a busy restaurant, we have you covered.

Local Service, National Support

As a locally owned and operated business, we’re committed to providing top-notch service to companies in Phoenix, backed by the strength of an established national brand.

Affordable & Cost-Saving

When you lease, you spread out the cost over a more extended period. Plus, the entire cost of the lease is a tax-deductible operating expense — making it a financially savvy decision.
